# Code of Conduct

This project adheres to the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) v2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
